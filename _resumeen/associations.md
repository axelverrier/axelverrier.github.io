## Scouting - Scouts et Guides De France • 2015-2021
**Organization and realization of solidarity and citizen projects**    
2021 - Helping to rebuild the village of Breil-sur-Roya (Alpes maritimes), following the storm Alex (2020), in partnership with the town hall and the local council community.      
2020 - Creation and implementation of an international project with the Emmaus Romania organization : the goal was to build a solidarity and educational farm to help the professional reintegration of long-term unemployed people. Establishment of partnerships, search for funding, management of a budget. Project postponed due to the pandemic.      
2019 - Mission of protection of Belle-Ile-en-mer island's fauna and flora in partnership with the Centre Permanent d'Initiatives pour l'Environnement.    
2018 - Participation in a bi-national meeting between scouts from France and Spain.  

Acquired skills: design and organize a project with institutional and associative partners. Fundraising and budget management.
